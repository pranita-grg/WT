Our Features

Child-Centered Learning

Focuses on the interests and needs of each child.

Prepared Environment

Materials are organized and Classrooms are designed to be engaging and accessible.

Focus on Life Skills

Practical life activities (e.g., cooking, cleaning) are integrated into the curriculum.

Mixed-Age Classrooms

Children of different ages learn together, fostering collaboration and peer learning.

Role of the Teacher

Teachers act as guides or facilitators rather than traditional instructors.
